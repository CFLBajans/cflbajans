Mum's Sweet bread

Bajan sweetbread is a crumbly, coconut flavored bread with bits of coconut and possibly raisins. Eat a slice as a snack, for dessert, or as toast for breakfast or pastry for tea!

Recipe submitted by a CFLBajan member

Servings: 3 loaves

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 cups of grated coconut
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 sticks of margarine
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 8 oz of coconut water with or without pulp
  • 1 tbsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp each of spice, cinnamon, ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp of each of mixed and vanilla essence
  • 1/2 cup of raisins coated with dry flour (optional)
  • 1 slightly beaten egg (optional)

Method

Prep time: 20mins
Ready in: 1 hr 20mins

  1. In a small bowl, mix together 1/2 cup of coconut, 2 tbsp of sugar, 1/4 tsp each of cinnamon, spice, ground nutmeg and 1/2 tbs essence.
  2. Mix the remaining ingredients together (except raisins) in another (larger bowl) until a firm dough is formed.
  3. Add raisins (optional).
  4. Divide dough in equal amounts for the loaf pans.
  5. Fill the greased loaf pans half full of dough, parting the center of the dough.
  6. Place a portion of mixture from small bowl in center of dough in each loaf pan. Fold the dough over the top to cover coconut mixture.
  7. Sprinkle top with a little sugar or glaze with a sugar and warm water mixture.
  8. Place pans in preheated oven at 350 degrees F.
  9. Bake for roughly an hour, until top is brown and the loaves are cooked. Test by piercing with skewer away from coconut mixture, dough is fully cooked if skewer comes out clean.
